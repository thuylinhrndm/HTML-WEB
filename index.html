Vietnamese Cuisine


The Vietnamese cook their food in a variety of ways, from deep fry, stir fry, boil to steam. The Vietnamese use a minimal amount of oil while cooking. Their purpose is to preserve the freshness and natural taste of food as much as possible. Vietnamese cuisine is often considered as one of the healthiest foods in the world.
